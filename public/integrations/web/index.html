Integrate Your Blog or Website With Nanr

With just one line of html you can add a Nanr button anywhere on the page. Just one click transfers a Nanr from the person clicking the button to you. Nanr's can be exchanged for cash - $0.18 per Nanr.

Did we mention that putting a Nanr button on your page is 100% free?

You can make the button as small as 40x40.

Or as large as you want!

Nanr can be used in addition to or instead of other monetization methods.

With Nanr you can directly monetize your great content.

Clicking on the Nanr button will never direct a user away from your page. In the event they click it and don't have an account a partial overlay will popup with a signup form. It features a prominent X in the top right which will immediatly close it if they deciade they would rather not create an account. Check it out below!
