Once you sign up, simply add the button to your blog/website. One click from a visitor transfers a Nanr from the visitor's stash to your stash.

Use Nanr to block your premium content until a minimum donation has been met. Minimum requirments can be specified on a per-page basis and Nanr can even work alongside an existing subscription or payment system.

Custom Integration

With just one line of javascript you can integrate Nanr into your website, blog or platform. Nanr will inform your system with the number of Nanrs a user has donated to you in total and on the current page.
